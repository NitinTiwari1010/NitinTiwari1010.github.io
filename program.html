Our Programs

NSSKalyanTrust.org.in runs various programs to support children and families in need. Here are some of our key initiatives:

  • Education

    Providing free education to underprivileged children to help them build a brighter future.

  • Skill Training

    Equipping youth with essential skills to improve their employability and self-reliance.

  • Nutritious Food

    Ensuring children receive healthy and balanced meals for their overall well-being.

  • Winter Essentials

    Distributing blankets, warm clothes, and other essentials to families during the harsh winter months.
