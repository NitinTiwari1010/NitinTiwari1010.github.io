About NSSKalyanTrust.org.in

NSSKalyanTrust.org.in is a non-profit organization based in Varanasi, UP, India. Our mission is to uplift children from low-income families by providing them with essential resources and opportunities to thrive.

Our Vision

We envision a society where every child has access to education, proper nutrition, and the skills needed to build a better future.

Our Mission

To support underprivileged children through education, skill training, nutritious food, and winter essentials.

Our Team

Our dedicated team of volunteers and staff work tirelessly to bring positive change to the lives of children and their families.
