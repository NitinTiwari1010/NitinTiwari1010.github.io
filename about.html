About NSS Kalyan Trust

NSS Kalyan Trust, established in 2023, is committed to uplifting society through education and social welfare programs. Registered under the Indian Trust Act, 1882, the trust is also recognized by NITI Aayog through NGO Darpan (Registration No. UP/2024/0417432).

Our Mission

To create a harmonious and empowered community where every individual has access to quality education, healthcare, and opportunities to lead a dignified life.

Our Vision

We envision a society where education, skill development, and social welfare programs empower individuals to achieve their full potential.

Objectives

  • Promoting education and awareness
  • Providing healthcare facilities
  • Empowering women, children, and disadvantaged communities
  • Environmental protection and public awareness
  • Alleviating poverty through essential resources
