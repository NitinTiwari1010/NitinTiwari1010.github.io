Welcome to Narayana Shaikshik Avam Samajik Kalyan Nyas (NSS Kalyan Trust)

NSS Kalyan Trust is a non-profit organization established under the Indian Trust Act, 1882. We are dedicated to empowering communities through education, skill development, and social welfare programs.

Our Focus Areas

  • Education & Awareness: We believe education is the foundation for a better future. Our programs focus on providing quality education, organizing awareness campaigns, and supporting underprivileged children to access learning opportunities.
  • Healthcare Facilities: Ensuring access to basic healthcare is a fundamental right. We organize health camps, provide medical aid, and work towards improving healthcare infrastructure in underserved areas.
  • Women and Child Empowerment: Empowering women and children is at the heart of our mission. We conduct skill development workshops, promote gender equality, and support initiatives that uplift women and children in need.
  • Environmental Protection: Protecting our planet is a shared responsibility. We engage in tree plantation drives, waste management programs, and campaigns to raise awareness about sustainable living practices.
  • Poverty Alleviation: We strive to break the cycle of poverty by providing vocational training, creating employment opportunities, and supporting marginalized communities to achieve self-reliance.

Who We Are

At NSS Kalyan Trust, we are committed to creating a positive impact in the lives of individuals and communities. Our mission is to foster growth, provide opportunities, and empower those in need through our dedicated programs and initiatives. With a focus on education, healthcare, and social welfare, we aim to build a brighter future for everyone.

Our Impact

Over the years, NSS Kalyan Trust has touched countless lives through its various programs. From educating underprivileged children to organizing health camps and empowering women, our efforts have brought hope and transformation to many. Together, we can achieve even more.
