Welcome to Narayana Shaikshik Avam Samajik Kalyan Nyas (NSS Kalyan Trust)

NSS Kalyan Trust is a non-profit organization established under the Indian Trust Act, 1882. We are dedicated to empowering communities through education, skill development, and social welfare programs.

Our Focus Areas

  • Education & Awareness
  • Healthcare Facilities
  • Women and Child Empowerment
  • Environmental Protection
  • Poverty Alleviation
